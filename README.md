### 🚀 Featured Projects

#### 📦 E-Commerce Business Intelligence Dashboard

[![Readme Card](https://github-readme-stats.vercel.app/api/pin/?username=asghar-dataanalyst&repo=ecommerce-analytics-project&theme=nord&border_color=00B4D8&title_color=00B4D8)](https://github.com/asghar-dataanalyst/ecommerce-analytics-project)

End-to-end analytics pipeline on a public UK e-commerce dataset (540K+ records) — built 5 PostgreSQL views with supporting indexes for CLV, cohort retention, and churn risk scoring, then designed a 3-page Power BI dashboard identifying the top 20% of customers driving 80% of revenue.

| AI-Assisted Workflow | Claude AI, DeepSeek |

#### ☕ Coffee Shop Analytics: Pricing & Portfolio Strategy

[![Readme Card](https://github-readme-stats.vercel.app/api/pin/?username=asghar-dataanalyst&repo=coffee-shop-analytics&theme=nord&border_color=00B4D8&title_color=00B4D8)](https://github.com/asghar-dataanalyst/coffee-shop-analytics)
